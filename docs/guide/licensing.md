# Licensing & use

This project is **open-core**: open to see, study, and use non-commercially, with
a clear paid path for commercial use.

## The three layers

| Layer | License | What it means |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Source code** | [AGPL-3.0](../../LICENSE) | Free to use and modify; if you run a modified version as a network service, you must release your source. Deters closed SaaS forks. |
| **Data & content** | [CC BY-NC 4.0](../../LICENSE-DATA.md) | Share/adapt for non-commercial use with attribution; commercial reuse needs a separate license. |
| **Upstream data** | World Bank CC BY 4.0 + OpenAlex CC0 | Reuse permitted; retain World Bank attribution. |

## Commercial options

The non-commercial data license is the lever. Commercial users can license:

- **Commercial data license** - an exemption from CC BY-NC for companies and
  governments wanting commercial reuse of the dataset.
- **Hosted / white-label** - managed hosting and branded dashboards for
  institutions.
- **Reports & consulting** - bespoke country/institution gap-analysis reports
  and advisory work.

## Publishing posture

The repository starts private and flips to **public** once the About/topics and
this documentation are finalized. Until then, in-app links to the repo guide may
404 for outside viewers.
